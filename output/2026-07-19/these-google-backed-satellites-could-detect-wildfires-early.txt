These Google-backed Satellites Could Detect Wildfires Early

Muon Space, which manufactures the craft, says they can detect fires as small as 16 x 16 feet, significantly smaller than existing satellites. Earth Fire Alliance and Muon Space plan for their full constellation to comprise over 50 satellites by 2030.
