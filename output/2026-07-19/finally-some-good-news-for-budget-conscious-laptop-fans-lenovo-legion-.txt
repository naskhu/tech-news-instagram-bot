Finally, some good news for budget-conscious laptop fans — Lenovo Legion R9000P is the world's first laptop with inkjet-printed OLED

Lenovo has launched the Legion R9000P as the world's first laptop featuring TCL CSOT's inkjet-printed OLED display technology. The gaming laptop introduces a 16-inch IJP OLED panel designed to combine OLED image quality with a newer manufacturing process.
