Watch this incredible time-lapse of the NASA Psyche mission’s Mars flyby.

The craft used Mars to get a gravity-assisted boost on the way to its namesake asteroid . The mission isn’t expected to intercept the asteroid until 2029, but in the meantime we’ve got this great view of Mars to enjoy.
