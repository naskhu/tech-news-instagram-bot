The White House is now deciding who gets access to frontier AI models, not the labs

The Trump administration is dictating access to frontier AI models, shifting that decision from Anthropic and OpenAI to the government via the Gold Eagle programme. Anthropic controlled access to its Mythos cybersecurity model through an initiative called Project Glasswing.
