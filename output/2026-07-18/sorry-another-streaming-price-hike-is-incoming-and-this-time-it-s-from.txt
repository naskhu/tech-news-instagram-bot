Sorry, Another Streaming Price Hike Is Incoming, and This Time It's From Apple

In the US, Apple Music's Individual plan jumped from $10.99 to $11.99 per month. Its Duo plan now costs $18.99 per month, up from $16.99, while its Family plan now costs $21.99 per month, up from $19.99.
