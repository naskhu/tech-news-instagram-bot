FCC plans to ban companies selling DJI products under other brands - Engadget

The proposed ban includes re-shelled versions of old DJI products. The Federal Communications Commission (FCC) wants to ban products from companies suspected to be selling rebranded and re-shelled DJI drones and cameras.
