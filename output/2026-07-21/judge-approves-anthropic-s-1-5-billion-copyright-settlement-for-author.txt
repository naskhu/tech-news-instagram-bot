Judge Approves Anthropic's $1.5 Billion Copyright Settlement for Authors

A federal judge has approved Anthropic’s record $1.5 billion settlement for an AI copyright lawsuit brought by a group of authors who claimed the company trained its AI models on pirated copies of their copyrighted works.
