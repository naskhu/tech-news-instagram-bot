Eight NATO allies launch HALO, a plan to link military satellites into one networked mega-constellation

Eight NATO allies have launched an initiative to connect their military satellites into a single networked mega-constellation, marking the Alliance’s most ambitious move yet to treat space as shared operational infrastructure rather than a collection of national assets.
