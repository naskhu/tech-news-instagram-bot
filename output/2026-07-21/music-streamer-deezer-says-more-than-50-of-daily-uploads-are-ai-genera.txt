Music streamer Deezer says more than 50% of daily uploads are AI-generated | TechCrunch

The number grew to 20,000 tracks, or 18% of daily uploads, in April 2025 . It then climbed to 30,000 tracks, representing 28% of daily uploads in September 2025, followed by 50,000 daily uploads, or 34% of daily uploads, in November 2025.
