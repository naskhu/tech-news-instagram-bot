deltaVision raises €10.2M to build the plumbing for in-orbit refuelling

Munich-based space startup deltaVision has raised €10.2 million in its first outside funding round, money earmarked for scaling production of the valves, pumps and pressure regulators that shuttle propellant around spacecraft.
