I gave ChatGPT’s new Work mode my most annoying life-admin tasks — and it handled them like a pro

Work mode is a new agentic mode for longer, more involved tasks that can research and analyze information across connected apps and files. Work mode is also better at one of the main bugbears of ChatGPT — running tasks at particular times.
