Judge Temporarily Pauses Paramount's Acquisition of Warner Bros. Discovery

As Variety reports , Judge Araceli Martinez-Olguin with the US District Court for the Northern District of California heard arguments on Friday and granted a 14-day restraining order blocking Paramount and WBD from closing the deal.
