Bucking EV slowdown, Sila raises $300M to expand battery materials factory

Battery materials startup Sila announced Tuesday that it raised $300 million to expand its factory in Washington State to produce enough anode material for more than 100,000 EVs. Sila previously announced deals to supply its anode material to Mercedes and Panasonic .
