Sila raises $300M to fast-track gigascale anode production and shore up US battery supply chains

Sila raised $300M to expand its Moses Lake silicon anode plant, pitching US-made battery materials as an alternative to Chinese supply chains. The company raised $375 million in June 2024 from investors including Coatue and Canada Pension Plan Investment Board.
