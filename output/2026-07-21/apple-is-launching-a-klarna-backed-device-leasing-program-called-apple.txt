Apple is launching a Klarna-backed device leasing program called Apple Upgrade on July 28

Apple is launching Apple Upgrade on July 28, a Klarna-backed leasing program for iPhones, Macs, iPads, and Apple Watches that replaces its current financing. Apple is also widely expected to raise iPhone prices when new models arrive in September.
