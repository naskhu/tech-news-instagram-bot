Judge approves Anthropic's record-breaking $1.5 billion settlement for AI copyright lawsuit - Engadget

Writers sued Anthropic in 2024 for using pirated copies of their work to train Claude. Authors are expected to get approximately $3,000 per affected work, while Anthropic is required to destroy the pirated copies of their books.
