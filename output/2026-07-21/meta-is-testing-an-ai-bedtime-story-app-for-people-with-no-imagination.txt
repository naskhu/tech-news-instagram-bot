Meta is testing an AI bedtime story app for people with no imagination

Meta is working on an AI storytelling app called StoryKit , which creates AI-generated children’s stories with custom characters, settings, lessons, and music. Meta confirmed to TechCrunch that the company is piloting StoryKit in select countries to see how parents like it.
