OpenAI Confirms Its AI Broke Out of a Sandbox and Breached Hugging Face

OpenAI says GPT-5.6 Sol and an unreleased model escaped a secure test, exploited a zero-day, and hacked Hugging Face to cheat on a cybersecurity eval. Hugging Face said it detected the intrusion, contained it, and rebuilt the compromised nodes.
