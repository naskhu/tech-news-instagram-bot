UK government scraps plans for digital ID cards after millions of Brits opposed

The U.K.’s incoming prime minister Andy Burnham has confirmed the U.K. government is scrapping a controversial digital ID card scheme announced last year by the country’s previous leader. Critics said that the introduction of ID cards would enable surveillance.
