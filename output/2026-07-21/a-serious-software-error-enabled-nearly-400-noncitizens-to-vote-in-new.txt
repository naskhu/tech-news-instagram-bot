A ‘serious software error’ enabled nearly 400 noncitizens to vote in New Jersey.

Fewer than 400 voted, and were registered across both parties or unaffiliated (for context, 154 million voted in the 2024 election). Governor Mikie Sherrill (D) ordered their removal from voter rolls and launched an investigation.
