Einride bets $38M on EV charging as it scales electric trucking | TechCrunch

Swedish electric and autonomous trucking company Einride has agreed to acquire EV charging startup Flipturn in an all-stock deal worth $38 million. Einride is perhaps best known for its unusual-looking cabless autonomous podlike trucks.
