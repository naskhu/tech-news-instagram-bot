Samsung launches its first US credit card with Barclays, its deepest move into finance yet

The Galaxy Card pays 5% back on Samsung purchases and lives entirely inside Samsung Wallet, as the company signals plans that run beyond a single co-branded card. There is no annual fee, according to Samsung.
