xMEMS unveils super tiny cooling fan that fits inside smart glasses - Engadget

Specifically engineered to fit inside a smart glasses temple arm, as well as inside AR and XR glasses, this tiny fan was built on xMEMS' µCooling platform . The XMC-1200 measures just 46mm², which makes it possible to integrate into tight spots.
