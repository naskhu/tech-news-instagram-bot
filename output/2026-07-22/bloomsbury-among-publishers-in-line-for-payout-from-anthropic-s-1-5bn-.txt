Bloomsbury among publishers in line for payout from Anthropic’s $1.5bn copyright settlement

Bloomsbury, the London publisher best known for the Harry Potter series, has confirmed it will collect a share of Anthropic’s $1.5bn settlement with authors, the largest copyright payout in US history.
