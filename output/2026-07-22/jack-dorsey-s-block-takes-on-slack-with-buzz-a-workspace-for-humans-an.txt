Jack Dorsey’s Block takes on Slack with Buzz, a workspace for humans and AI agents

Block, the Dorsey-led fintech behind Square and Cash App, has released Buzz , a free, open-source platform for humans and AI agents to work together. Multiple agents and multiple humans share a workspace and build on each other’s work.
