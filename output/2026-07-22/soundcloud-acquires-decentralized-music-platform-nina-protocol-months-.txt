SoundCloud acquires decentralized music platform Nina Protocol months after its shutdown | TechCrunch

SoundCloud announced Wednesday that it has acquired Nina Protocol, a decentralized music platform that enabled independent artists to sell music directly to fans while keeping 100% of their revenue. It’s also unclear whether any members of Nina’s team will join SoundCloud.
