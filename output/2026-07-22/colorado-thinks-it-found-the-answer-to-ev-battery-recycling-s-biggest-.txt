Colorado thinks it found the answer to EV battery recycling's biggest challenge - Engadget

A Colorado law signed in June (SB26-003) makes automakers responsible for collecting and processing unwanted EV batteries. Used EV batteries contain valuable minerals like lithium, nickel and cobalt — all of which are recyclable.
