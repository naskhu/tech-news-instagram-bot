Europe’s EV market share tops 25 percent for the first time as June registrations surge nearly 40 percent

The June data shows that momentum has not faded even as fuel prices have partially retreated. Germany led the volume figures with 84,057 battery-electric registrations in June, giving EVs above 28 percent of new car sales in Europe’s largest automotive market.
