Suspicious content? NordVPN's new AI bot will check it for you on WhatsApp, X, and Instagram

Today, NordVPN announced the launch of NordBot, an AI-powered agent designed to detect suspicious content across social media and messaging apps. By evaluating content on the fly, NordBot aims to stop users from falling victim to fake giveaways and AI-generated imagery.
