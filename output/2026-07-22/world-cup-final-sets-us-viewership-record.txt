World Cup final sets US viewership record.

The match was up 132 percent compared to the 2022 final — averaging 16,784,000 viewers — and up 240 percent over 2018’s final which drew 11,441,000 viewers. For perspective, about 125.6 million Americans watched the most recent Super Bowl.
