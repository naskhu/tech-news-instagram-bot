Origin Energy investigates potential breach of customer data

Origin Energy, Australia’s largest electricity and gas retailer, told the Australian Securities Exchange on 22 July that it is investigating potential unauthorised access to some of its customers’ data. The company has promised a further update once its investigation firms up.
