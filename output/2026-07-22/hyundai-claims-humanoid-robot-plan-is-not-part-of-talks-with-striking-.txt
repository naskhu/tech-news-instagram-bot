Hyundai claims humanoid robot plan is not part of talks with striking workers

Hyundai Motor Company’s plan to put humanoid robots to work by 2028 is not part of current negotiations with striking South Korean autoworkers, according to the company. The Hyundai Motor union is a branch of the Korean Metal Workers’ Union.
