Big Tech’s AI spending is catching up with its cash flow

Epoch AI estimates that hyperscaler capex is expanding at roughly 70% a year while operating cash flow grows about 23%, which puts aggregate spending on track to overtake operating cash flow around the third quarter of 2026.
