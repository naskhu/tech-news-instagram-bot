Oracle says one AI data centre could cost it a $7bn power guarantee

In a sworn affidavit , Oracle says the rules would force it to post a letter of credit “exceeding $7 billion,” at an annual cost that “could exceed $100 million.” The Public Service Commission of Wisconsin tightened the terms in May.
