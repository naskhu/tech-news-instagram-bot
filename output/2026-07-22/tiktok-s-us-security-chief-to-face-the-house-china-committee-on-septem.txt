TikTok’s US security chief to face the House China committee on September 15

The committee, chaired by Republican John Moolenaar, intends to press Farrell on data privacy and on how much control ByteDance, TikTok’s Chinese founder, still holds over the American company. The hearing also arrives amid a wider appetite for TikTok testimony.
