Light Flip: the $299 anti-AI flip phone with no touchscreen

Light, the Brooklyn maker of deliberately boring phones, has unveiled the Light Flip, its first clamshell and its fourth device in a decade. The Light Flip runs LightOS, the company’s stripped-back operating system, showing white text on a black screen.
