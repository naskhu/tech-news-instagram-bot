‘ChatGPT for Doctors’ Mulls New Financing at $20 Billion Valuation

OpenEvidence, the fast-growing startup that helps doctors find medical information through an AI chatbot, has considered raising $200 million after receiving offers from investors at a valuation of around $20 billion, according to a person involved in the discussions.
