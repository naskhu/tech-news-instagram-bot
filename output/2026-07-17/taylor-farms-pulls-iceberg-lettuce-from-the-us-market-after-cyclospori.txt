Taylor Farms pulls iceberg lettuce from the US market after cyclosporiasis outbreak

This all follows statements from the CDC and FDA saying the “explosive diarrhea parasite” outbreak has been linked to shredded iceberg lettuce served at Taco Bell locations across five states: Indiana, Kentucky, Michigan, Ohio, and West Virginia.
