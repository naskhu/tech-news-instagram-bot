After spending a month with the Mission 1 Pro I think it could be the ultimate vacation camera if you pick up the point-and-shoot grip — I just wish it was better for stills

The GoPro Mission 1 Pro is a more premium action camera than previous GoPros, with 8K/60p recording capabilities and better low-light performance thanks to its new sensor. The downside of all this is that the Mission 1 Pro doesn’t come cheap.
