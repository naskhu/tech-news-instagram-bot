Sony's FX5 cinema camera finally offers open gate and RAW 5K recording - Engadget

The FX5 slots between the FX3 and FX6 in the company's "Cinema Line" and comes with features previously missing on Sony cameras like RAW video and open gate recording. With a new de-squeeze function, it also allows easier use of anamorphic lenses.
