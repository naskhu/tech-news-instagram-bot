Nvidia’s open-source simulator trains surgical robots in under two minutes

By running 8,192 training environments in parallel on GPUs, Nvidia claims a startling speed-up. Surgical-robotics firms including Medtronic, Johnson & Johnson MedTech, and CMR Surgical are using the framework for surgical digital twins and endovascular training.
