China narrows the AI gap with the US to a record-low 6%

Chinese models narrowed the performance shortfall to a record-low 6% in June, down from 9% in May, according to Bloomberg Intelligence . Chinese models took two slots in LiveBench’s global ranking of large language model performance last month.
