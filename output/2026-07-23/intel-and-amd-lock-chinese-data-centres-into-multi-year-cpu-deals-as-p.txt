Intel and AMD lock Chinese data centres into multi-year CPU deals as prices surge

Intel and AMD have signed longer-term supply commitments with Chinese server customers as data centre CPU prices climb, according to Reuters, which cited two people familiar with the talks. Chinese customers would typically commit to about a year of supply.
