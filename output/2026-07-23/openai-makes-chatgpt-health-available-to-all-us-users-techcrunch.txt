OpenAI makes ChatGPT Health available to all US users | TechCrunch

OpenAI said today it is making ChatGPT Health, a feature that helps users with health-related queries, available to all U.S.-based users over 18 across all plans. OpenAI said earlier that users needed to use the health hub for health-related queries.
