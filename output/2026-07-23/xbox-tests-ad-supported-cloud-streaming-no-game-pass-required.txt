Xbox Tests Ad-Supported Cloud Streaming, No Game Pass Required

Microsoft is testing ad-supported Xbox games, giving consumers a free way to play in exchange for viewing a commercial. Microsoft didn't specify which devices are supported, but the Xbox Insider program is available on Xbox consoles and Windows 10 and 11 PCs.
