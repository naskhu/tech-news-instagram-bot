MeBeMe's new ‘interrupter’ app helps you become your best self instead of doomscrolling | TechCrunch

MeBeMe is a new app that acts as an “interrupter,” designed to help users become their best selves instead of getting lost in endless scrolling. Under “Mind,” users can choose roles like mathematician, scientist, historian, or chess player.
