Jeff Bezos has been approached to buy a stake in Liverpool FC. Amazon already holds the Premier League TV rights.

Jeff Bezos has been approached to join a consortium seeking to buy a 30% stake in Liverpool Football Club from Fenway Sports Group, Sky Sports reported. The provisional offer is £1.35 billion ($1.8 billion), valuing Liverpool at approximately £4.5 billion ($6 billion).
