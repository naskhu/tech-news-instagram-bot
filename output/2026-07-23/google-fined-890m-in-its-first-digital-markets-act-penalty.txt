Google fined €890M in its first Digital Markets Act penalty

It is Google’s first sanction under the DMA, though not the regime’s first. Google was designated a gatekeeper in 2023 for core services including Search, Android, Chrome, and Google Play, the label that brought Thursday’s obligations to bear.
