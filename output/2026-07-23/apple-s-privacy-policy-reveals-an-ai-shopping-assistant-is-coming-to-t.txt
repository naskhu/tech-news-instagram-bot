Apple’s privacy policy reveals an AI shopping assistant is coming to the Apple Store app

Apple’s updated privacy policy for the Store app reveals a coming “Virtual Shopping Assistant.” It collects chat, device, and location data. The setting is found under Account > Settings in the Apple Store app.
