Ring has upgraded two of its cameras and the new peephole model looks like it'll be perfect for apartment-dwellers — its no-drill installation won't damage your door, so it's ideal for renters

The new Ring Outdoor Camera (2nd Gen) is available in both wired and battery versions. Ring says its camera is tuned for low-light conditions, but this camera doesn't see in the dark like the Outdoor Camera does.
