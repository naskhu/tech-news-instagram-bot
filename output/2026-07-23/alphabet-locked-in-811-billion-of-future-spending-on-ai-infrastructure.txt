Alphabet locked in $811 billion of future spending on AI infrastructure. The number jumped $500 billion in one quarter.

Alphabet had $811 billion in contracted future spending commitments at the end of June, up nearly $500 billion from just three months earlier. Cloud revenue hit $24.8 billion in Q2, up 82% year-on-year, providing the revenue engine to service the commitments.
