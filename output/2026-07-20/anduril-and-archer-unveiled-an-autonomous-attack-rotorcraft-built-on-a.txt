Anduril and Archer unveiled an autonomous attack rotorcraft built on air taxi technology. First flight is planned for 2027.

Anduril and Archer unveiled Thunder at Farnborough, an autonomous attack VTOL built on a dual-use air taxi platform. Archer has already completed multiple test flights with full-scale surrogate aircraft.
