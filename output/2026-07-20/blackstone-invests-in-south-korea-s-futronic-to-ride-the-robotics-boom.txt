Blackstone invests in South Korea’s Futronic to ride the robotics boom

Eugene Cook, who runs Blackstone’s private equity business in Korea, called Futronic “the very best of Korea’s mechatronics industry,” pointing to its research and engineering depth. What Blackstone will pay, and how much of Futronic it will end up owning, remain unstated.
