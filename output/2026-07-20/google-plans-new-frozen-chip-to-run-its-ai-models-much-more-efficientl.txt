Google Plans New ‘Frozen’ Chip to Run Its AI Models Much More Efficiently

Google is working on a new server chip that would directly integrate the blueprint of its Gemini AI model, enabling the company to serve its AI models to users much more efficiently, according to two people with direct knowledge of the matter.
