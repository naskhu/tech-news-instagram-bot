A British AI lab signed up Nvidia, Meta and Samsung to invent materials that don’t exist yet

CuspAI screened 300 trillion possible molecular structures and returned 20 validated candidates, it said . The backers frame the work as a shift in how science gets done. “CuspAI built a search engine” for undiscovered materials, said Josh Coyne of Kleiner Perkins.
