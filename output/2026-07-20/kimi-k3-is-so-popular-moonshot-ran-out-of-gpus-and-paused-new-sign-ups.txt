Kimi K3 is so popular Moonshot ran out of GPUs and paused new sign-ups

Its new Kimi K3 model proved so popular that it ran out of computing power to serve everyone. User requests in the two days after launch ran far past Moonshot’s own projections, it said, taking the compute cluster close to full.
