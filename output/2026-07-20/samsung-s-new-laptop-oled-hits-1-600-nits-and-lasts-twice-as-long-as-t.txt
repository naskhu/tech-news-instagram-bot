Samsung’s new laptop OLED hits 1,600 nits and lasts twice as long as the panel it replaces

Samsung Display is shipping tandem OLED laptop panels reaching 1,600 nits peak brightness, 40% above single-stack OLED, with twice the lifespan. Samsung says the tandem panel lasts more than twice as long as a single-stack OLED under typical laptop operating brightness.
