Neo exits stealth with $100M from a16z and Bessemer to build a control layer for agentic AI software

Neo raised $100M from a16z and Bessemer to build a real-time control layer for agentic AI software in enterprises. Neo’s platform covers AI agents, AI-enabled applications, browser extensions, MCP servers, plugins, and traditional software acquiring agentic features.
