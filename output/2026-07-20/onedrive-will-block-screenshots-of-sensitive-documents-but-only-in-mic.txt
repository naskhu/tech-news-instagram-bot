OneDrive will block screenshots of sensitive documents, but only in Microsoft’s own browser.

Microsoft warns that users opening protected documents outside Edge “ may receive inconsistent enforcement ” and recommends that businesses use Conditional Access or browser-management policies to direct employees toward Edge.
