Starlink's 'Mini 2' Dish Appears in Firmware With Built-In Battery

New Starlink firmware indicates that SpaceX has started producing a “Mini 2” dish, which features a built-in battery and a sign that it supports even lower power consumption. So it looks like the company may have renamed it the Mini 2.
