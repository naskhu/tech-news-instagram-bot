Bentley hired an orchestra to solve the EV silence problem. Drums were the answer.

Bentley’s Torcal EV uses a “Dynamic Symphony” performed by real musicians on drums, viola, and bass guitar. Whether a viola can make a two-tonne electric SUV feel like a 1930s Blower Bentley is a question only a test drive will answer.
