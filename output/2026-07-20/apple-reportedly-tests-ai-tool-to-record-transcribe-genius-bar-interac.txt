Apple Reportedly Tests AI Tool to Record, Transcribe Genius Bar Interactions

Apple is reportedly testing an AI tool to record, transcribe, and summarize conversations between customers and its Genius Bar employees. Live Notes is reportedly a pilot program intended to make life easier for Genius Bar employees.
