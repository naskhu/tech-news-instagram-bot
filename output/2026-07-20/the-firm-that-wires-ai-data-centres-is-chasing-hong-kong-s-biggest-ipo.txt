The firm that wires AI data centres is chasing Hong Kong’s biggest IPO since Alibaba.

Zhongji Innolight, a Chinese maker of high-speed optical transceivers, has started gauging demand for a Hong Kong listing worth as much as $8bn. At $8bn, the listing would be Hong Kong’s largest first-time share sale since Alibaba raised $12.9bn in 2019.
